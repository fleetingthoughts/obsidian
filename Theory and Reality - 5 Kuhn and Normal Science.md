- Broad ideas of traditional science challenged. Two points both contrasted against popper:
	- Science is not openly always trying to challenge like Popper but is instead very closed minded
	- Science is not one contiguous phase encouraged by one "Recipe:, it occurs in phases and each phase operates under different standards
- Kuhns way of argumentation appeals to history a lot which is vastly different from positivists/popper. Specifically he linked questions about the philosophy of science to questions about history of science
- Difficult to distinguish betweeen normative and descriptive claims about science
- One paradigm per field at a time

	- consensus forging ()
