Abbreviated as (NMA), this is an update to [Mozart Critical Editions - Neue Mozart Ausgabe (NMA)](Mozart%20Critical%20Editions%20-%20Neue%20Mozart%20Ausgabe%20(NMA).md) and follows the [Kochel Catalogue (K.V) - Mozart](Kochel%20Catalogue%20(K.V)%20-%20Mozart.md).

The complete series are the following
1. Sacred Vocal Works (15 volumes)
2. Stage Works (26 volumes)
3. Lieder, Part Songs, Canons (3 volumes)
4. Orchestral Works (19 volumes)
5. Concertos (14 volumes)
6. Church Sonatas (1 volume)
7. Music for Large Ensembles of Solo Instruments (3 volumes)
8. Chamber Music (11 volumes)
9. Piano Music (7 volumes)
10. Supplement (ca. 25 volumes)

Of course vol. 9 is the relevant edition to my piano education
