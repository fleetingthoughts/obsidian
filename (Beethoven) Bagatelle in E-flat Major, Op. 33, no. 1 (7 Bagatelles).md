The **Bagatelles**, Op. 33, for solo piano were composed in 1801–02 and published in 1803 through the Viennese publisher __Bureau des arts et d'industrie__. The seven Bagatelles are quite typical of Beethoven's early style, retaining many compositional features of the early Classical Period
