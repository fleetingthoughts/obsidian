wrongful act or infringement of right not under contract requirements:
- duty of care
- breach of care
- damages
Torts:
- negligence: not meet standard of care
- nuisance: damages involve interference even recreational. Proof of negligence not required
- defamation
- trespass
Negligence requirements:
- duty of care
	- duty to warn
- breach of duty
- actual damages suffered
- actual causal link
Product liability: a type of tort. No contract between customer and company

common issues in contract/tort law. Tort law 
- concurrent liability. duty to client but also duty to public from the impacts of your product (e.g. bridge doesn't collapse on public)
- limitation period - two years except quebec which is 3
- ultimate limitation - 15 years.
- joint and several reliability - each stakeholder can be held liable
