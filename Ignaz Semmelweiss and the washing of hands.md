Ignaz Semmelweis had attempted to show that the mortality rates of babies and the mothers can be reduced through the washing of hands. His propositions were initially rejected and derided pushing Semmelweis to the mental breaking point and was confided to an asylum late in his life.

Its worth noting that its not as if doctors never washed hands. They were washed with whatever soap and water was available at the time, but Semmelweis pushed for a more thorough washing with a chlorinated lime solution.
