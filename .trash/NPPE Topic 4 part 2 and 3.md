# Bonds and Construction Liens
- Bond agreement
	- surety (bond company/insurance): guarantee provider
	- obligee (project owner): require guarantee
	- principal (construction company): performing obligation
	- one-sided where surety must provide guarantee for obligee

	- subcontractors/contractors can make construction liens
	- holdbacks (usu 10%).  holdback 10% held until project milestones complete
