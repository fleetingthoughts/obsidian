A  technique for bowed strings indicated by a tenuto or staccato under a slur. This is commonly seen in piano works as well and the intent of the composer is to emulate that sound by the strings. Commonly great composers will adopt the habits and indicators they apply for the compositions of non-piano works and are thinking of the grander world of music even in their piano compositions and wish to emulate a non-pianistic sound or technique
