This is a 4-volume edition of the complete Haydn Piano Sonatas published by Breitkopf & Hartel
This edition of Haydn is an instructive edition based on the original [critical edition by Breitkopf & Hartel](Breitkopf%20&%20Hartel%20Edition%20of%20Joseph%20Haydn.md) with fingerings and suggestions by the pianist, composer, and conductor Hermann Zilcher who per the preface aimed to find a balance between the research and the need to produce music. The fingerings were described by him in his preface to follow the "Haydn Style".
