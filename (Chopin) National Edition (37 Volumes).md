Referred to as the Chopin National Edition or the Polish National edition, it is an urtext edition of Chopin's complete work that aims to represent it in the most authentic form. This edition is produced by the publishers and people behind the prestigious International Chopin Piano Competition and is the edition recommended to the contestants.

The edition was started by Jan Ekier in response to the [[Paderewski edition]].
