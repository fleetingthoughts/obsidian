For urtext editions:
- Gesamtausgabe (Henle). Various editors depending on the volumes. For instance Gunter Thomas edited several of his minuets and marches while Georg Feder edited the complete sonatas
- [Breitkopf & Hartel Edition of Joseph Haydn](Breitkopf%20&%20Hartel%20Edition%20of%20Joseph%20Haydn.md). An aborted series that was not completed but does contain the sonatas and reprinted interpretive editions (see below)
Interpretive editions:
- [Joseph Haydn Piano Sonatas (Hermann Zilcher ed.)](Joseph%20Haydn%20Piano%20Sonatas%20(Hermann%20Zilcher%20ed.).md) that contain fingerings and suggestions by an actual piano performer and musician based on the urtext editions published by [Breitkopf & Hartel](Breitkopf%20&%20Hartel%20Edition%20of%20Joseph%20Haydn.md).
