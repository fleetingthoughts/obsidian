# Law for Professional Practice
- Provincial vs Federal
	- Can only appeal upward
	- supreme court is highest court of appeal
- Common Law vs Statute law vs case law
	- Statute law is actual law established by legislation
	- common law similar to case law is based on precendent
	- Common law not used in quebec except in federal
		- Operates on civil code and codified statues
- Claims and disputes
	- common in construction
# Types of law
- private vs public
	- public affect societal institutions
	- private is between individuals
- criminal vs civil
	- civil involves compensation
	- criminal law involves punishment
# elements of contracts
1) offer and acceptance
2) intent to accept
3) consideration: both parties benefit 
4) within mental capacity to accept
5) legal purpose: must be legal

- waiver and estoppel
	- waive a legal right
	- estoppel: cannot reinforce something on principle that was previously waived
- quantum meruit: value for work done. Compnensation for unseen circumstances.
